## üîí Discovery Limits

To maintain a healthy and safe community, certain features are limited by your current level and verification status.

### 1. Discovery Radius
- **Lvl 1:** Limited to **Campus Mode** only. Standard university directory access.
- **Lvl 2+:** Access to **City Mode**. Discovery across multiple local campuses (up to 100km).
- **Lvl 4+:** Access to **Room Mode** (Live 100m proximity). Requires **Elite Verification**. *Note: In this mode, you only see and are seen by other verified students.*

### 2. The Elite Verification Gate (Level 4+)
To protect the community during live proximity interactions, reaching Level 4 triggers a mandatory security check. You must complete these steps to unlock Room Mode and continue progressing to Level 5:

1.  **Student Email (SSO/Code):** Proves you belong to the university.
2.  **Phone Number (SMS):** Links your account to a verified device.
3.  **Photo Identity Verification:** Upload a selfie/ID. Our algorithm verifies your identity within minutes to ensure a safe, bot-free environment.

### 2. Social Interactions (Daily Limits)
*These limits reset every 24 hours at midnight.*

- **Friend Invites:**
  - Standard: 10/day
  - **University Verified:** 25/day
- **Profile Pings:**
  - Standard: 5/day
  - **University Verified:** 15/day

### 3. Visibility Boosts
- **Profile Boost:** Verified users get a persistent **5x visibility boost** in the discovery feed.
- **Level Boost:** Each level after Lvl 3 adds a stacking **5% weight** to your profile's ranking in global searches.

### 4. Meetup Hosting & Visibility
*The scope and size of your gatherings grow with your reputation.*

- **Friends Mode (Lvl 1+):** Only visible to your mutually connected friends.
- **Campus Mode (Lvl 1+):** Visible to every student at your specific university.
- **City Mode (Lvl 2+):** Visible to students across all local colleges (e.g., if you are at NYU, students at Columbia can see it too).

#### Level-Based Hosting Capacity:
- **Lvl 1:** Max 5 attendees.
- **Lvl 2:** Max 10 attendees.
- **Lvl 3:** Max 25 attendees.
- **Lvl 4:** Max 50 attendees.
- **Lvl 5:** Max 100 attendees.
- **Lvl 6:** Unlimited capacity + Featured on the main feed.
